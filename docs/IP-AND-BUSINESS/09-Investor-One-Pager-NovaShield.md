## THE ONE SENTENCE

NovaShield is the first integrated police accountability platform to give every American a composite Officer Accountability Score for any tracked officer, real-time community safety alerts about police activity, verified incident documentation that holds up in court, and legal rights guidance — all in one privacy-first platform that never shares data with law enforcement.

---

## THE PROBLEM

**45 million Americans** have a direct police encounter every year. Millions more live in communities where police misconduct is chronic and under-reported.

When you encounter a police officer in America today, you have no way to:
- Know the officer's complaint history or accountability record
- Get a real-time alert that there's elevated police presence in your neighborhood
- File a misconduct report that has any evidentiary weight
- Verify whether your complaint was cross-referenced with court records or FOIA data

Civil rights attorneys, investigative journalists, and major civil rights organizations have teams to gather this information. **Individual citizens have nothing.**

**That gap is the platform.**

## THE PRODUCT

**NovaShield** — a police accountability platform built with the same rigor that financial platforms apply to fraud detection and credit scoring: structured, verified, searchable, and actionable.

| Feature | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Officer Accountability Score (0–100)** | Composite algorithm scoring each tracked officer from community reports, sustained complaint ratio, severity weights, temporal decay, and FOIA cross-reference. Risk-classified: Low / Moderate / High-Risk |
| **District Accountability Score (0–100)** | Geographic district-level scoring aggregating officer metrics, complaint density, resolution rates, and reform progress |
| **Community Safety Alert Network** | Real-time alerts about police sweeps, elevated presence, and checkpoints — generated by community citizens, distributed to community subscribers |
| **Verified Incident Pipeline** | NS-YYYY-### case ID system; 4-stage verification (Submitted → Under Review → Verified → Resolved); FOIA cross-referencing; court-formatted document export |
| **Anonymous Documentation** | File reports with zero personal identifiers; platform never discloses data to law enforcement |
| **Interactive Incident Map** | Severity-coded geographic visualization of incidents + district boundaries + proximate legal resources |
| **Know Your Rights Guide** | Jurisdiction-aware legal rights by encounter type (pedestrian, traffic, home) |
| **Legal Resources Directory** | ACLU, Legal Aid, NAACP LDF, NLG — contact details, service types, geographic proximity matching |
